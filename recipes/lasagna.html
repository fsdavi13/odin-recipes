Here you're going to learn how to make the best lasagna in the world

Description

To make a classic meat lasagna, start by cooking chopped onion and garlic in olive oil, then add ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork) and cook until browned.
Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar, and let the sauce simmer until flavorful. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese
with an egg, grated Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, then add a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by the
ricotta mixture, shredded mozzarella, and more sauce. Repeat the layers until the dish is full, finishing with sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan on top. Bake in a preheated
oven at about 180 °C (350 °F) for around 35-45 minutes until bubbly and golden, then let it rest for about 10 minutes before slicing and serving.

Ingredients List

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 °C (350 °F).
  2. Cook onion and garlic in olive oil, then brown the ground meat.
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings; simmer the sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta, egg, Parmesan, and parsley in a bowl.
  5. Layer in a baking dish: sauce → noodles → ricotta mixture → mozzarella.
  6. Repeat the layers and finish with sauce and cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 35-45 minutes, let it rest for 10 minutes, then serve.
